Operability

“the ability to keep an equipment, a system or a whole industrial installation in a safe and reliable functioning condition, according to pre-defined operational requirements”
  • Does all this as well as being declarative, convergent, effectively immutable

What Bosh does for you

  1. Create all the VMs
  2. Auto-healing
  3. Rolling upgrades
  • Bosh does amazing, magic things compared to trad ops

Stage 1: Denial

“the survivor imagines a false, preferable reality”
  1. Why are we using a thing that no-one else does?

Learning Points

Remember that Bosh gives you:

  1. Declaration of intent
  2. Effectively immutable
  3. IaaS agnostic
  4. Highly automated
  5. Self-healing
  6. Open source and extensible(ish)
  1. There isn't an easy answer - just remember what it does for you

Terminology

Jobs are made up of templates

Templates for a job are in /var/vcap/jobs

  1. Jobs in releases are templates in a manifest, which are jobs on the VM, which are instance of a job. Not to be confused with a Monit job, or a Bosh task or Bosh errand.

Stage 3: Bargaining

“the survivor seeks compromise”
  1. Subverting Bosh mitigates its benefits - eg using Chef, replacing Monit

Learning Points

  1. Do not argue with Bosh

Stage 4: Guilt

“the survivor believes to have committed sin”
  1. Boshed infrastructure is supposed to last
  2. Unlike other tools - everyone else must scale the cliff

Learning Points

  1. Same as any niche technology choice
  2. Radiate reasons for using it
  3. Pair on release development

Stage 5: Depression

“the survivor has feelings of severe despondency and hopelessness”

Learning Points

  1. Tooling is not great, do not expect it
  2. Use bosh-lite
  3. Consider a pool of Boshes for CI
  1. Testing is hard - black box acceptance tests only
  2. Each director only does one thing at a time, CI bottlenecks

Stage 6: Acceptance

“a calm, retrospective view for the survivor, and a stable set of emotions”
  1. Stockholm syndrome - everyone over the cliff loves it
  2. Fundamental philosophical truths (declarative, convergent, immutable)
Must be good if it has a parody

Learning Points

You now have:

  1. IaaC
  2. A team capable of end-to-end continuous delivery

Stage 7: Hope

“the survivor develops a positive attitude about the future”
  1. Joke: off-by-one errors
  2. bosh.io
  3. CCP allows zero-cost trial of releases, and downloading of them
